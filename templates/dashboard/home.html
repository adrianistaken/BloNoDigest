Your weekly ritual — do this before each Thursday

The robot collects events automatically; your job is to review the good ones and hit send. Roughly 30–60 minutes.

  1. 1

    Collect

    On Sources, click “Run import” on each source (or let the nightly job do it).

  2. 2

    Review

    On Events → This weekend, approve (✓) the good ones, reject (✗) junk.

  3. 3

    Generate

    On Digests, click “Generate draft for upcoming weekend.”

  4. 4

    Polish

    In the draft, reorder, move sections, edit blurbs, or remove events. Check the live preview.

  5. 5

    Send

    Click “Send test” to email yourself first, then “Send to subscribers.”
